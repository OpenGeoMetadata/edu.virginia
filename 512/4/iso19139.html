Abstract:

* Variant Tile: Carte particuliére de l'Amérique Septentrionale
* Author: Popple, Henry
* Credit: Area Table 7 1741 Popple
* Published: at Amsterdam : Printed for I. Covens and C. Mortier, [1741]
* Relief shown pictorially.
* Pictorial insets at top of sheet: Falls of Niagra -- Mexico -- Québec -- New York.
* Insets along right border: Placentia -- Annapolis Royal -- Boston -- New York -- Charleston -- Bermuda -- S. Augustine -- Providence -- Havana -- S. Jago -- Kingston -- Port Antonio -- Port Royal in Martinica -- Barbados -- Antigua -- Cartagène -- Porto Bello.
* Identified as the 1st state of Dutch Key Map (Covens and Mortier) by Mark Babinski. See his Popple website at the University of Southern Maine Library.
